Bug Reporting

If you believe you have experienced a bug, please email us to let us know

When reporting a bug, please include the user, the time and date, operating system and browser.

Then detail the series of steps that lead up to the issue - please be as specific as possible here.

Also include a screenshot if appropriate. You can take a screenshot on a Mac using cmd+shift+4. Screenshots on a Windows machine vary depending on the version of the operating system, see eg https://goo.gl/7u4P9i for examples of how to do this.
